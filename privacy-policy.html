Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Jan Sverre Bauge collects and uses information in connection with our Facebook integrations and jansverre.net.

We do not collect personal data from visitors through our Facebook integration. The integration is used solely to publish content on our Facebook page (JSLiberalist). We do not store information about Facebook users.

We use the Facebook Graph API to publish posts on our Facebook page. We only access our own page data and do not post on behalf of other users. We do not store Facebook user data.
